Overview:

There are a number of different software systems that have grown up over the years and while there has been some convergence we seem to have reached some kind of steady state in many laboratories. It seems a good time for some "cross fertilisation" or even "hybridisation".....

The aim of this "workshop" is to compare and contrast the different annotation software packages that genome centres use to see what we do well and less well and what we can learn and improve on and see if there are areas where we could help each other.

The format of the workshop will be short demonstrations and talks on the annotation systems attendees use followed by discussions as a group on important issues of mutual interest.


Prerequisites:

Attendees should be people who are either experienced users or experienced developers of the annotation software they have brought along.
