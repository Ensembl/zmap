Overview:

There are a number of different software systems that have grown up over the years and while there has been some convergence we seem to have reached some kind of steady state in many laboratories. It seems a good time for some "cross fertilisation" or even "hybridisation".....

The aim of the workshop was to compare and contrast the different annotation software packages that genome centres use to see what they did well and less well, what we could learn and improve on and to look for possible collaborations in important areas of mutual interest.

The format of the workshop was short demonstrations and talks on the various annotation systems followed by discussions as a group on important issues of mutual interest.
